Camera Movement
WASD key inputs move you around the space
Press W to move forward
Press A to move left
Press S to move backward
Press D to move right

Mouse movement will change where the camera is looking

The camera will move to where you are looking at. If you wish to move above the level to a specific look with your camera up and press W. If you wish to lower your self to a specific location look down and press W.
